Objective:
The objective of Minesweeper is to clear the board without detonating any mines. You win the game by revealing all tiles that do not contain mines.
Gameplay:
Starting the Game:
Left-click on any tile to reveal it. If the revealed tile contains a mine, you lose the game.
Revealing Tiles:
Each revealed tile will display a number indicating the number of mines adjacent to it. Use this information to deduce the location of mines. If a tile has no adjacent mines, it will be blank, and all adjacent tiles will automatically be revealed.
Flagging Mines:
Click on the "Flag" button to enable flagging mode. In flagging mode, clicking on a tile places a flag.

Winning the Game:
To win the game, reveal all tiles that do not contain mines. Once all safe tiles are revealed, you win!

Losing the Game:
If you reveal a tile containing a mine, the game ends immediately, and you lose. Avoid detonating any mines by carefully revealing tiles and flagging potential mine locations.
